At a glance (from the map data)

This is a point-to-point route, so plan the return — retrace your steps or check local buses; with only 112 m of climbing, it's one of the gentler options in the area for older dogs or hot days; there is one mapped water point on the route — carry enough to bridge the gaps; for a break, you'll pass Park Cafe and Kröss among others.
