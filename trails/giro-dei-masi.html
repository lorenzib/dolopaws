At a glance (from the map data)

This is a true loop — it closes back on its own start point, so there's no return leg to plan; the 228 m of climbing is steady rather than steep — a fair ask for most fit dogs; water is well covered, with 8 mapped points along the way; for a break, you'll pass Dolci Note and Nikka's among others.
