At a glance (from the map data)

This is a true loop — it closes back on its own start point, so there's no return leg to plan; the 307 m of climbing is steady rather than steep — a fair ask for most fit dogs; no water points are mapped on this route, so carry everything your dog will drink; for a break, you'll pass Manaka Bar and Araucaria among others.
