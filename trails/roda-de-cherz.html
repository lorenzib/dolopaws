At a glance (from the map data)

This is a true loop — it closes back on its own start point, so there's no return leg to plan; with only 110 m of climbing, it's one of the gentler options in the area for older dogs or hot days; no water points are mapped on this route, so carry everything your dog will drink; for a break, you'll pass Ütia Cherz.
