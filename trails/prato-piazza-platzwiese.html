Prato Piazza (Plätzwiese)

Low-risk DoloPaws route-audited

A loop from the Prato Piazza meadows up the old military road past Rifugio Vallandro to the panoramic summit of Monte Specie (Strudelkopf, 2307 m), returning across the plateau on the Alta Via 3 path — views to Tre Cime, Monte Cristallo and a WWI-era fort along the way.
