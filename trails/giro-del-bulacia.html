Giro del Bulacia

Moderate 🐾 Verified by DoloPaws Paid access

GPS-verified closed loop (Waymarked Trails relation 9483250) near Bulaccia in the Sciliar-Catinaccio park, officially marked as a "PU" trail. Confirmed dog-friendly via an independent hiking blog account of the route done together with a dog. Passes Tschötsch Alm, Rifugio Puflatsch, and the Panche delle Streghe (Witches' Benches) viewpoint.

Good to know

There's an electrified cattle gate partway up — approach calmly and guide your dog through rather than letting them investigate it. Skip this trail if there's heavy snow cover (avalanche risk on some sections). Elevation profile and exact water-source/rifugio positions along the km-marks are not yet verified — treat as approximate.
