At a glance (from the map data)

This is a true loop — it closes back on its own start point, so there's no return leg to plan; 816 m of climbing makes this a big physical day — for conditioned dogs only, with turn-back discipline; no water points are mapped on this route, so carry everything your dog will drink; for a break, you'll pass Chalet Biollet.
