Sentiero del Trenino

Low-risk Verified by DoloPaws

GPS-verified path (Waymarked Trails relation 9445694) along the old Val Gardena narrow-gauge railway line (1916–1960), connecting Ortisei, Santa Cristina, and Selva. Historical information boards throughout; a restored railway tunnel is walkable near Santa Cristina. Official Val Gardena tourism source rates it "facile" (easy) with no particular safety risks, and it's explicitly described elsewhere as ideal to walk with dogs.

Where to start

Start here — can begin from Ortisei, Santa Cristina, or Selva di Val Gardena

Good to know

Very gentle, wide, stroller/bike-accessible in parts — a great low-effort option. Multiple access points along the valley floor, so you can walk just one section if you don't want the full 12km.
