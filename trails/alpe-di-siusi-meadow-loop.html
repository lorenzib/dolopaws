Alpe di Siusi Meadow Loop

Low-risk DoloPaws route-audited

A real, GPS-verified loop from Compatsch using trails 7, 6, and 30 — confirmed by multiple independent hiking guides as wide gravel and paved paths, suitable even for all-terrain strollers. Europe's largest high-alpine meadow, gently rolling and wide open.

Good to know

Multiple entry points; go early to find free parking, or take the cable car up from Ortisei/Siusi. This loop skips the longer Saltria detour some guides include via trail 9 — it's the shorter, easier variant of that bigger circuit.
