Before you go — the basics

  • Dogs must stay on a lead on Italian trails.
  • Cable cars will usually ask for a muzzle to be carried.
  • Carry more water than you think — routes above the treeline often have none.
  • Weather turns fast above the treeline — start early on busy weekends.
